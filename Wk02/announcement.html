What to Expect This Week

This week you will have another reading assignment and a quiz. Just like last week, you will need to pass the quiz with 100% in order to move on to week 3. You can take it as many times as you need, but do it by the deadline or the late penalty will apply.

This week you will also be starting on your site plan for the fictitious white water rafting site. Planning is an essential step when creating a website. You will come up with the site colors and fonts as well as determine what the purpose of the site is and who the site is for. Some questions to ask yourself when working on this assignment are "Why does this company want a website?"" (purpose) and "Who will visit this website?" (audience).

What to do When You Have Questions / Need Help

As you go through these assignments, you may have questions or need help with things. It's always great to ask your peers using the questions and conversations discussion board. When asking questions, be sure to include screenshots and code examples. Because of the nature of this class, it is hard to help without examples. You might also want to include some of the things that you have tried so far to fix your problem.

Teaching one another is an important part of the learning model. Check the questions and conversations board frequently. Ask questions there and answer your peers questions if you can! If you ask a question and are able to figure it out on your own, come back and post the solution so that others with the same question are able to find the answer.

Of course, reaching out to me individually is also an option. I can be reached via phone, text, email, whatsApp, and messaging within i-Learn.
