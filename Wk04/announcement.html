Getting Help and Feedback on Assignments

Discussion Boards

I have gone through the course and added a questions and conversations discussion board to every week of the course. These discussion boards are not required. but can be used to ask and answer questions and even share things you've learned. Please take some time to go through this board and see if you can help any of your peers who have asked questions.

Don't forget to include screenshots and code examples when asking for help.

If you post your website on this board, your peers can review it. If you post it by Thursday at 7 mountain time I will review it during office hours and give you some feedback.

Tutoring

Tutoring is available for free through BYU-Idaho. If you are struggling with an assignment I recommend getting a tutor to help you.

You can visit this website to schedule a tutoring session: https://iplan.byui.edu/enterprise/login.html?redirectUrl=https%253A%252F%252Fiplan.byui.edu%252Fenterprise%252F

If you need help navigating the website and getting a tutor, here's a video showing how to do it: https://video.byui.edu/media/PC+I-Plan+Tutor+Sign-up+Tutorial/0_43d3eldx

Ask me!

If you don't feel comfortable posting your question to the discussion board or would rather talk to me directly, please, reach out! I am here to help you. I can't help you if you don't ask. You can reach me via email, messaging in canvas, call, text, and WhatsApp.

Please include screenshots and code examples when asking for help. I also need to know what specifically you're struggling with and what you have already tried.
