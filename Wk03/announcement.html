What to Expect This Week

This week you will have another Khan Academy lesson and will learn about what it means to be a web designer. You will also be learning more about wireframes and how to troubleshoot your code. You will also have another activity this week to continue working on your white water rafting site. In this activity you are going to be writing the HTML for the home page by following the wireframe that was provided. There isn't as much guidance in the instructions as there was last week and you will be expected to write most of the HTML on your own. I know this sounds challenging but you are ready for it!

I want you to keep a few things in mind:

  1. The assignment will make more sense if you refer to the wireframe while reading the instructions
  2. Your assignment will NOT look like the wireframe at the end of the assignment. If you watch the videos in the wireframing activity (please do) it shows a pretty good example of what your site will look like this week.
  3. Don't forget to complete the wireframing and troubleshooting activities. It's important you understand what is taught in those lessons.

Grading and Late Work

I do my best to grade assignments within a timely manner. Most of the time I grade your work within a day of you turning it in. If you turn in your work early and get less than 100% I try to tell you how to improve your work and allow you to resubmit by the deadline for full points. So if you turn your work in a little early, you have an advantage! I have been grading assignments on Saturdays as they come in, but once quarantine is over I will likely not grade assignments on Saturdays.

Late work is only worth 1/2 points so be sure to get your work in on time. That includes re-submitted work.
